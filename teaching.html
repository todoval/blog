Thesis Supervision

I am open to supervising both Bachelor’s and Master’s theses in the field of Computer Graphics.
Potential topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Light Transport Simulation
  • Spectral Rendering
  • Color Science
  • High Dynamic Range (HDR) Imaging
  • Volume Rendering
  • Camera Calibration

Students with their own project proposals related to these areas are also welcome to reach out.
